DREAMS

a random musing related to consciousness using parent / child structures

parent => outline
children => colors 

outline + colors => what we know well or think we know well. "colors" here can mean anything from experimenting to mastery to fuzzy understanding.

unseen outline + colors => what we know well from habits, bias, muscle memory, etc. this is more like a transition from outline + colors, we retain the colors but forget the outlines

outline + no colors (what we are coloring) => what we are learning, conventions we are following (like how to be a good girlfriend)

dreams are colors with little outline and those outlines would be what seeps in during the day.
